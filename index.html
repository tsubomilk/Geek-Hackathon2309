What's the Onsen?

Hot water from the Earth

Hot springs are warm waters that spring from deep within the earth. There is hot magma inside the Earth, which heats the groundwater. The warm water springs to the surface and we can enjoy hot springs. It is the heat of the earth that creates this special natural phenomenon.

Health benefits

Hot springs are perfect for relaxing your body and getting rid of fatigue due to their rich mineral content and warmth, and they also help keep your skin beautiful. Additionally, soaking in the warm water of a hot spring improves blood circulation and helps relieve muscle and joint pain.

Unique experience

You can enjoy a special time soaking in a hot spring while admiring the beautiful scenery of a hot spring resort that can only be found in Japan. By staying at a traditional Japanese hot spring inn, you can enjoy local specialties and cuisine, and experience Japanese culture and hospitality.

YoroKeikoku Onsen

Yorokeikoku Onsen in Chiba is a quiet hot spring area surrounded by beautiful nature. You can enjoy a luxurious time soaking in a hot spring while listening to the sound of a mountain stream. Outdoor enthusiasts can also enjoy hiking and nature walks.

Ikaho Onsen

Ikaho Onsen in Gunma is famous for its 365 stone steps, the number of which is equal to the number of days in a year. Enjoy the hot springs flowing directly from the source and experience the atmosphere of a hot spring town. You can balance relaxation and sightseeing.

Hottarakashi Onsen

Hottarakashi Onsen in Yamanashi is a quiet hot spring area surrounded by a beautiful natural environment. You can enjoy the view of the mountains while soaking in the bathtub. You can even watch the sunrise! It's the perfect place to spend some time relaxing and pampering.

Onsen Manners

Don't soak towels or hair

Enter the hot spring naked. Do not put towels in hot springs. Tie your hair to prevent it from getting into the water.

Wash your body

Wash your body before taking a bath. Let's go into the hot water in order from the feet. Make sure there are no people around before pouring hot water.

Respect the atmosphere

Lots of people relax in hot springs, so enjoy the environment. Please do not disrupt the environment by talking loudly.
