Top 3 Onsen around Tokyo

No.1 Kusatsu Onsen

SteamyDelight, BlissfulSerenity

Kusatsu Onsen in Gunma is
known for its beautiful hot springs
fed directly from the source and
its high-temperature hot springs
with beautiful natural scenery.
Enjoy a relaxing moment while
enjoying a footbath in the hot spring field.

No.2 Hakone Onsen

Nature's Embrace, Paradise of Beauty and Tranquility

Hakone Onsen in Kanagawa
is located at the foot of Mt. Fuji
and is home to a historic hot spring town
and many art museums.
Discover more than just hot springs in this place!

No.3 Kinugawa Onsen

Harmony with the Rapids, Enchanting Hot Springs Journey

Kinugawa Onsen in Tochigi is
a historic hot spring resort
where you can enjoy a beautiful valley walk
and hot springs in one place.
It is a calm and healing place.

Onsen Manners

Enter the hot spring naked. Do not put towels in hot springs. Tie your hair to prevent it from getting into the water.

Wash your body before taking a bath. Let's go into the hot water in order from the feet. Make sure there are no people around before pouring hot water.

Lots of people relax in hot springs, so enjoy the environment. Please do not disrupt the environment by talking loudly.
